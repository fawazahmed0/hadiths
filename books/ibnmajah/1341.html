Bengali

। আবূ হুরাইরাহ (রাঃ) থেকে বর্ণিত। তিনি বলেন, রাসূলুল্লাহ সাল্লাল্লাহু আলাইহি ওয়াসাল্লাম মসজিদে প্রবেশ করে এক ব্যক্তির কুরআন তিলাওয়াত শোনেন। তিনি জিজ্ঞেস করেনঃ কে এই ব্যক্তি? বলা হলো, আবদুল্লাহ ইবনু কায়েস। তিনি বলেনঃ তাকে দাউদ (আলাইহিস সালাম)-এর সুমধুর কন্ঠস্বর দান করা হয়েছে।

English

It was narrated that Abu Hurairah said:“The Messenger of Allah (ﷺ) entered the mosque and heard a man reciting Qur’an. He asked: ‘Who is this?’ It was said: ‘(He is) ‘Abdullah bin Qais.’ He said: ‘He has been given (sweet melodious voice) from the Mazamir of the family of Dawud.’”

Indonesian

Telah menceritakan kepada kami [Muhammad bin Yahya] berkata, telah menceritakan kepada kami [Yazid bin Harun] berkata, telah memberitakan kepada kami [Muhammad bin Amru] dari [Abu Salamah] dari [Abu Hurairah] ia berkata, "Ketika Rasulullah shallallahu 'alaihi wasallam masuk ke dalam masjid, beliau mendengar bacaan Al Qur`an seorang laki-laki. Beliau bertanya: "Siapa ini?" dikatakan, "Dia adalah Abdullah bin Qais. " Beliau bersabda: "Orang ini telah diberi seruling dari seruling keluarga Dawud

Turkish

Ebu Hureyre (r.a.)'den; şöyle demiştir: Resulullah (Sallallahu Aleyhi ve Sellem) mescid'e girdi ve bir adam'ın Kur'an okuduğunu işitti. Bunun üzerine: «Bu (okuyucu) kimdir?» diye sordu. Abdullah bin Kays (r.a.)'dir denildi. Efendimiz: «Gerçekten bu adam'a Davud (Aleyhisselam)'ın mazmurlarından bir mazmur (nağmelerinden bir nağme) verilmiştir.» buyurdu. Not; Zevaid'de şöyle denmiştir: Ben derim ki bu hadisin aslı Buharl ve Müslim'de Ebu Musa (r.a.)'ın hadisindendir. Müslim'de ayrıca Büreyre (r.a.)'ın hadisindendir. Nesai'de ise Aişe (r.a.)'ın hadisindendir. Ebu Hureyre (r.a.)'ın hadisindeki isnadın ricali sika zatlardır

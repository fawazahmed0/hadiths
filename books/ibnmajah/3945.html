Bengali

। আবূ বাকর সিদ্দীক (রাঃ) থেকে বর্ণিত। তিনি বলেন, রাসূলুল্লাহ সাল্লাল্লাহু আলাইহি ওয়াসাল্লাম বলেছেনঃ যে ব্যক্তি ফজরের নামায পড়লো, সে আল্লাহর যিম্মায় থাকলো। অতএব তোমরা আল্লাহর যিম্মাদারিকে নষ্ট করো না। যে ব্যক্তি তাকে হত্যা করবে, আল্লাহ তাকে তলব করে এনে উল্টো মুখে জাহান্নামে নিক্ষেপ করবেন।

English

It was narrated from Abu Bakr Siddiq that the Messenger of Allah (ﷺ) said:“Whoever offers the morning prayer, he is under the protection of Allah, so do not betray Allah by betraying those who are under His protection. Whoever kills him, Allah will seek him out until He throws him on his face into Hell.”

Indonesian

Barangsiapa membunuhnya, maka Allah akan menuntutnya sehingga melemparkan wajahnya ke dalam neraka

Turkish

Ebu Bekr-i Sıddık (r.a.)'den rivayet edildigine gore; Resulullah (Sallallahu Aleyhi ve Sellem) şöyle buyurdu, demiştir: «Kim sabah namazını kılarsa o kimse Allah'ın guvencesindedir. Artık Allah'in bu guvencesini ihlal etmeyiniz. Bu itibarla kim bdyle olan bir kimseyi (şer'i hiikum olmaksızın) öldürürse Allah öldüren kişiyi sorguya gekip neticede baş aşağı cehennem'e atar.» Not: Zevaid'de şöyle denilmiştir: Bu sened, ravileri güvenilir olmakla beraber munkati' (kopuk)tur. Çünkü ravi Habis Sa'd bin İbrahim, ravi bin Sa'd'e yetişmemiştir. Bu durumu Tehzib sahibi söylemiştir

Bengali

জাবির ইবনু আবদুল্লাহ (রাঃ) হতে বর্ণিত আছে, তিনি বলেন, রাসূলুল্লাহ সাল্লাল্লাহু আলাইহি ওয়াসাল্লামের নিকট আমরা কোন সময়ই মৃত্যুর শপথ করিনি, বরং আমরা তার নিকট যুদ্ধক্ষেত্র হতে পালিয়ে না যাওয়ার শপথ করেছি। সহীহ, মুসলিম ১৫৯১ নং হাদীসটি উল্লেখ হয়েছে। এ হাদীসটিকে আবূ ঈসা হাসান সহীহ বলেছেন।

English

Narrated Jabir bin ‘Abdullah : "We did not pledge to the Messenger of Allah (ﷺ) for death, but only that we would not flee." [Abu 'Eisa said:] This Hadith is Hasan Sahih, meaning both of the Ahadith are Sahih. Some of his Companions pledged to him for death, they said only: "We will not leave from in front of you as long as we are not killed." While others pledged him by saying: "We will not flee

Turkish

İbn Ömer (radıyallahü anh)’den rivâyete göre, şöyle demiştir: Rasûlüllah (sallallahü aleyhi ve sellem)’e söylediklerini dinlemek, dinlediklerimizle de yaşayıp itâat etmek üzere biat ederdik ve bize: “Gücünüz yettiği konularda” buyurdu. (Nesâî, Biat: 18) Bu hadis hasen sahihtir. İkisi de mana yönünden aynı olup sahihtir. Şöyle ki: Ashabından bir kısmı; kendisine ölüm üzere biat ederek sağ olduğumuz sürece yanında olacak ve beraber hareket edeceğiz demişlerdir. Diğer bir kısmıda her konuda birlikte olacağız ve kaçmayacağız diye biat etmişlerdir

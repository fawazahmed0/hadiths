English

Abdullah b. Shaqiq reported:I said to'A'isha (Allah be pleased with her): Did the Messenger of Allah (ﷺ) fast for a full month besides Ramadan? She said: I do not know of any month in which he fasted throughout, but that of the month of Ramadan and (the month) in which he did not fast at all, till he ran the course of his life

Indonesian

Telah menceritakan kepada kami [Yahya bin Yahya] telah mengabarkan kepada kami [Yazid bin Zurai'] dari [Sa'id Al Jurairi] dari [Abdullah bin Syaqiq] ia berkata, saya bertanya kepada [Aisyah], "Apakah Nabi shallallahu 'alaihi wasallam pernah berpuasa selama satu bulan penuh secara terang-terangan selain di bulan Ramadlan?"
